Concerning the Artist Life

There has been a growing worry about the job security of artists, due to AI and the decrease in demand for art. While this may be the case, there are many reasons that artists will continue, and why I want to continue in this career.



Why is this so?

  1. The diversity of Jobs artists can still do that AI cannot
    • AI is still unable to create comic strips where the characters look the same in multiple frames
    • Animation that makes sense and flows well
    • There is an increase in Youtube Artists that are bringing their art to the world
  2. There now a lot more ways to market Art
    • Due to the emergency of the Internet, it is much more easier for people to get their art out there
    • I'm planning on majoring in Marketing, which will allow me to learn how to market my comics to other people and increase the reach it can get
  3. Regardless of monetary gain...
    • Artists will still do their art for their own happiness and share it with the world
    • There will always be people who appreciate it
